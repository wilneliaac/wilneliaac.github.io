Norms and Expectations for Distance Learning
  • Be on time and present the entire class period
  • Use your correct name when joining class
  • Be prepared to learn (mentally and with materials)
  • Mute your microphone unless you are permitted to speak
  • Turn on your webcam and position your camera properly. Your face should be well-lit and visible the entire class period unless otherwise directed
  • Attend class from a dedicated and well-lit work space
  • Dress and present yourself appropriately for school
  • Chats should be used for class-related communications only
  • Engage in active listening
  • Raise your hand to be recognized to avoid competing conversations
  • Students should immediately contact the tech team for any technology issues
